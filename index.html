About Us

Student-led, community-driven, competition-ready.

FTC 23928  ·  2025–2026 Season  ·  Inspire Award

Double Quarter Pounders (FTC 23928) is a student-led FIRST Tech Challenge team based in Northern Virginia, founded in 2023. We design, build, and program competition robots while inspiring the next generation of innovators through hands-on outreach.

Through robotics, mentorship, and community events, our students gain real-world engineering experience that develops innovation, leadership, and lifelong learning — on and off the competition field.
